About me

Hi, my name is Tsan, I was born in Hunan, China and I am currently in Melbourne for my pilot training.

I was profoundly hooked by flying since I was a child, so I decided to take participate in the pilot recruitment and pretty lucky to be admitted to China Eastern Airline. I am a KSP and GTA5 player. I like a variety of sandbox and simulation games.
